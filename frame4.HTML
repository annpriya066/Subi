FRAME 4

Electronegativity is a measure of the tendency of an atom to attract a bonding pair of electrons. As we move across a period from left to right the nuclear charge increases and the atomic size decreases, therefore the value of electronegativity increases across a period in the modern periodic table and decreases down the group.
